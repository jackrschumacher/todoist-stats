üîí Privacy & Security

Your data privacy is the top priority. Here is how this app handles your information:

  • Client-Side Only: Your Todoist API Token and task data are stored locally in your browser's localStorage.
  • Direct Communication: When you sync, your browser communicates directly with the official Todoist API. Your task data is never sent to or stored on any intermediate server.
  • Anonymous Stats: We collect aggregate, anonymous counters (e.g., "Total Tasks Processed Globally") using Firebase to show the community stats bar. No personal identifiers or task contents are ever uploaded.
